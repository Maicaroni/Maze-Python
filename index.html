Available Commands

You can use the following commands to move the bunny around the maze.

for i in range(n):
function()
end

range(n) - the "n" represents the number of times you want the bunny to move.
function() - is the command you want the bunny to execute.
end - is used to signify the end of the loop.


    Functions you can use:

  • move_up() - Move the player up

  • move_down() - Move the player down

  • move_left() - Move the player left

  • move_right() - Move the player right
